PCOS Diet Tips

  • Choose low glycemic index (GI) foods to manage insulin levels
  • Include protein in every meal to stabilize blood sugar
  • Eat fiber-rich foods (vegetables, whole grains, legumes)
  • Limit refined carbs, sugary foods, and processed items
  • Stay hydrated with 8-10 glasses of water daily
  • Eat smaller, frequent meals rather than 2-3 large ones
  • Include anti-inflammatory foods (turmeric, berries, fatty fish, nuts)
